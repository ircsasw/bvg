Bay View Grand Cancun

Bay View Grand Cancun is an extensive and exclusive residential
development facing the Caribbean Sea.


Location in Cancun

Located in one of the most privileged, Hotel Zone with the beautiful
and an amazing Beach, is also just minutes from
the airport and the Downtown, schools and cultural malls of Cancun.

Residences

Offers 215 residences on 2 and 3 levels lots starting at 500 m2 to 1000 m2
where you can enjoy amazing Beach view.


Services

With most sophisticated services and amenities, a place to make your life enjoyable
without leaving development with activities like reading workshop, karate, yoga,
dance, spinning, language classes or simply tour the extensive green areas, Concierge
Service, Security and having fun with new movies in the Movie Theatre.
